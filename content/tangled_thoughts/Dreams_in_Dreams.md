What if you slept?
And what if,
in your sleep,
you dreamed?
And what if,
in your dream,
you went to heaven
and there plucked a strange and beautiful flower?
And what if,
when you awoke,
you had that flower in your hand?
Ah, what then?
 
~ Samuel Taylor Coleridge
