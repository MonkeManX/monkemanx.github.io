Meister Eckhart was a Christian German mystic from the 14th century. This book contains selected sermons by him. One major theme in almost all of these sermons is that God is beyond all predicates. Whenever you describe God: for example, by saying He is all-powerful or all-good—you do Him a disservice, because you limit Him. In fact, God is beyond all descriptions.
