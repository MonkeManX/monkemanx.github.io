> A cardinal asked St. Bernard,
> 'Why should I love God and in what manner?' St. Bernard replied, 'I
> will tell you. God is the reason why we should love Him. The mode is
> without mode.' For God is nothing: not in the sense of having no
> being. He is neither this nor that that one can speak of: He is being
> above all being. He is beingless being. Therefore the mode of loving
> Him must be modeless. He is beyond all speech. That we may come
> to this perfect love, may God help us. Amen
>
> ~ Meister Eckhart, Sermon Q 82
