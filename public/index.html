Neovim Cheat Sheet

Neovim Cheat sheet This is a short cheat sheet for important keybindings in vim more specific for neovim. Capitalization is important! Modes: ESC, switch to normal mode. v, switch to visual mode. i or a, switch to insert mode. R, replace mode. Navigation: arrow keys, shame on you! Movement keys, can be combined with arbitrary number h, to the left. l, to the right. j, downwards. k, upwards. Example: 10j jumps 10 lines downwards, 5k jumps 4 lines upwards.
